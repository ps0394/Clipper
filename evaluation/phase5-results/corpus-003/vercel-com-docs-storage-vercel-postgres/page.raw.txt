Vercel lets you connect external Postgres databases through the 
Marketplace
, allowing you to connect external Postgres databases to your Vercel projects without managing database servers.

Vercel enables you to use Postgres by integrating with external database providers. By using the Marketplace, you can:

Select from a 
range of Postgres providers

Provision and configure a Postgres database with minimal setup.

Have credentials and 
environment variables
 injected into your Vercel project.
